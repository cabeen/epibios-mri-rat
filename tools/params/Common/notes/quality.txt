This directory contains rodent data from the EPIBIOS project.  The data is
organized into different levels of analysis.  Here's a short guide to what
each level: 

  level0: raw data archives as received (copied from ftphome)
  level1: organized into directories of scanner data like ${site}/${subject_id}
  level2: nifti data, "raw" for direct conversion, "common" for renamed files
  level3: image analysis results
  level4: statistical analysis results

Notes on data quality
=====================

Some Finland data has inconsistent DWI baseline intensities, e.g.

	20180413_BIOS_1146_9d.MS1 has a mean intensity of 1385.00
	20180504_BIOS_1155_30d.Nd1 has a mean intensity of 3461.63

It is not clear whether this is intensity scaling or if the acquisition
changed between these two scans in a way that affects SNR.  If the latter is
true, then we have to figure out a way to denoise the data consistently.  If
the former is true, then we just have to find the scaling parameter so that
the denoising bandwidths are comparable.  A third option is to compute the
denoising bandwidth on a per-scan basis, e.g based on the global mean and std
of the image.
